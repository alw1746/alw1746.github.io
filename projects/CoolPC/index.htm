How I Built My Quiet and Cool PC


My early PC setup was noisy and CPU temperature approached 70°C under 100% load.

  • Gigabyte GA-MA74GM-S2H motherboard
  • AMD Athlon 64 X2 5000+ (dual-core 2.6Ghz)
  • Standard CPU fan from manufacturer
  • Generic 350W power supply
  • Seagate 3.5" SATA 120Gb hard disk

To fix this annoying problem, I did the following...

1. Replace the stock fan with an after-market cooler.

Cooler Master Geminii S
2. Install a quality low-noise var speed fan power supply.
Corsair VX450W
3. Use a quiet 2.5" 5400 RPM SATA laptop hard drive.
Western Digital Scorpio hard drive
4. Remove all covers and elevate PC to improve airflow.
