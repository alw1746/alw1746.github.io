Arduino Pro Mini with NRF24L01+ 2.4Ghz radio


The nRF24L01+ is a cheap ($5 AUD) 2.4Ghz radio transceiver module on ebay with a data transfer rate of 250kbps, 1Mbps and 2Mbps over a SPI interface. It runs on 3.3V but the i/o pins are 5V-tolerant which makes it easy to interface to an Arduino Pro Mini.



A 47uF bypass capacitor is used to smooth out power fluctuations that could affect its operation. Some recommend using a 100nf decoupling cap as well to filter out high frequency noise in the power. All the cheap ebay modules are clones of the original Nordic Semiconductor chip but good enough for hobbyist use.



The NRF24L01+ has a peak transmit current of 115mA so a stable power supply from a 500 mA USB port is good. A 250 mA 3.3V LDO regulator steps the 5V down for the transceiver.
